# A cleaner template for automatic generation

You can also standardize everything with this template:

**[Route actions], then find [target parking space] and park.**

Examples:

* Go straight, then find an empty parking space and park.
* Turn left, then find a parking space on the left and park.
* Go straight, then turn right, then find a parking space on the right and park.

This format is very good for:

* dataset generation
* label parsing
* compositional generalization experiments
* language consistency control
